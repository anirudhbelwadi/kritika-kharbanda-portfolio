At Uyuni de Salar, the landscape is equivalent to a pirate’s treasure. Visiting the Salar is a unique sensory experience. However, the visitors restrict the sensory experience by mobilizing only the visual stimulus. Therefore, we created an escalier that merges into the landscape, born out of the landscape itself. The luminous journey takes the visitor through multiple arenas, with varied materials stimulating the sensory experience. ‘The Liminal’ is a holistic architectural device that gives an opportunity to the visitors to orchestrate a combination of senses, communicating with them intellectually, psychologically, emotionally and spiritually.
