The task of this assignment was to create a conceptual visualization of one's body motion. We selected the motion of a violinist, and used the app Fologram for smartphone (Android) for tracking the motion. The selected theme was classical, and our team selected three classical pieces with varying tempo, ranges and frequency.
Piece 1 - Beethoven
Key: F major; Range: G3-A6; Time signature: 2/2; Tempo: 66BPM

Piece 3 - Mozart
Key: G Minor; Range: D4-D6; Time signature: 4/4; Tempo: 150BPM

Piece 2 - Johann Pachelbel(Canon)
Key: C Major; Range: C4-C6; Time signature: 4/4; Tempo: 70BPM The recorded motion data was exported as a CSV file. Early explorations with abstract geometry were done. Further, we used algorithms for extracting velocity and acceleration, and mapped the motion using spheres. Lastly, point relaxation algorithms were explored to observe results.

Classical Consciousness

Deconstructing the kinetics of a violinist
