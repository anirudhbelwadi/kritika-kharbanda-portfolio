Visionary concepts and creative imageries together lead to architectural innovations’’ exhausting resources and thriving demands have compelled us to overcome the imaginative resistance. Therefore, new realms of architecture are explored in an attempt to re-interpret the reality. Architecture is not just about catering the immediate needs, but also about anticipating the future challenges. A design should have the potential to bring about a revolutionary change. In today’s world, where the notion of showcasing every moment and capturing it for the future, caused by social media is trending, spaces which provide people an opportunity to do so are relevant. the pavilion above ground attracts people from all over the state, coming into the bus terminal, which is situated below the ground. Shifting the transit zones of the city below the ground, which will eventually lead to the formation of a twin city that is underground, will provide greeener spaces above.

The Beauty of Indeterminance:
The pavilion appeals to the residents and non-residents alike, because of its curvilinear form which blends effortlessly into the surroundings. it stands tall and gracefully in contrast to the surrounding structures, as an avante garde system of the modern society, yet reflecting the character of the city. The ferrocement sheel structure is supported by steel trusses, and a central core that links the superstructure with the substructure. the core carries all the required services and lifts for vertical circulation. Planes at varied levels inside the pavilion hold a variety of functions which encourage the participation of locals and provide a chance for employement generation for the nearby labour.
